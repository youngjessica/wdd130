I hope to be able to create with them in my future career. My dream job would be to work for company as like a digital organizer or presenter. If someone gave me files of information and visual assets such as logos and images and asked me to create a slide presentation or visual aids for their website or presentation, I'd be in career heaven!

I look forward to learning with you all this semester. Good luck!
